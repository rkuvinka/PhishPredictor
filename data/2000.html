Friday, 05/19/2000
Key Club, Los Angeles, CA

Set 1: Funky Bitch, My Soul, JibbooGotta Jibboo, First Tube, Bug, Heavy Things, Twist > Tube > Piper -> Llama, Brian and Robert, Magilla

Teases:
· Dave's Energy Guide tease in Bug
· Theme from Jeopardy! jam

Notes: This set was broadcast on the Mark and Brian radio program. Funky Bitch and My Soul were performed as a warm-up before the show went on the air, although the crowd was already in the bar. Studio versions of Birds, Bouncing, and Heavy Things were used as intros and outros for the commercials.
