Saturday, 09/06/2008
Midtown Loft and Terrace, New York, NY

Set 1: SuzySuzy Greenberg, Julius, Waste

Notes: This impromptu reunion took place at Brad Sands’s wedding reception. This was the only known performance by Phish during the “break-up”.
